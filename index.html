Rock, Paper, Scissors Game

Objective:

The player competes against a computer, which randomly selects a move. The goal is to choose a move that beats the computer’s choice.

How to Play:

Click Rock, Paper, or Scissors to play against the computer.
Or use your keyboard: *R* for Rock, *P* for Paper, *S* for Scissors.
The computer randomly generates one of the three moves.
The winner is determined by the following rules:

  • Rock crushes Scissors → Rock wins
  • Scissors cut Paper → Scissors win
  • Paper covers Rock → Paper wins
If both the player and the computer choose the same move, the round is a tie, and a new round begins if desired.
